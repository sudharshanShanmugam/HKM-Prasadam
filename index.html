About Us

About HKM Chennai

Hare Krishna Movement Chennai is dedicated to propagating the teachings of Lord Sri Krishna as presented in the Bhagavad Gita and Srimad Bhagavatam. Our mission includes serving sanctified prasadam (food offered to Lord Krishna) to thousands of people every day.

Through our Annadanam program, we serve nutritious and delicious prasadam to devotees, visitors, and the underprivileged, believing that no one within a 10-mile radius of the temple should go hungry.
